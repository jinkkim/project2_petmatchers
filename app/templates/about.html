    PET CARE COST

Many animal adopters say they decide to adopt a dog or a cat because they just fell in love with them at first sight or they made an eye contact with the animal. Adopting an animal from an animal shelter is great but sadly many of them return to the shelter again. We want potential adopters have general ideas about available animals from 10,000+ shelters in north America and the cost of own an animal and want them to make informed decision on animal adoption.


Factors should be considered: animal breed, coat color, gender, age, house-trained, vaccinated, size, spayed/neutered, location, good with other dogs/cats, good with kids
