    PET CARE COST



Approximately 6.5 million companion animals enter U.S. animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3.3 million are dogs and 3.2 million are cats. When you make a pet adoption decision, those factors could be considered: animal breed, coat color, gender, age, size, house-trained, vaccinated, spayed/neutered, location, good with other dogs/cats and good with children.
