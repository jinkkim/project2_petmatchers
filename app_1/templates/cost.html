    PET CARE COST



Many animal adopters say they decide to adopt a dog or a cat because they just fell in love with them at first sight or they made an eye contact with the animal. Others adopters are looking for companions for pets they may already own. Adopting an animal from an animal shelter is great but sadly many of them are returned to the shelter again. Unexpected costs is the trending reason for returning adopted pets to shelters. These unexpected costs are incurred due to the following “Top 3 Reasons”: Health issues and illness can result in costly emergency veterinary care, Underestimated cost for necessities: food, capital expenditures, care costs, Behavioral issues can result in costly training fees. We want potential adopters to know the cost of own an animal and want them to make informed decision on animal adoption.
